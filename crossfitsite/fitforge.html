About CrossFit

CrossFit is a fitness program that was created by Greg Glassman in 2000. The program combines a wide variety of functional movements into a timed or scored workout. CrossFit is designed to increase your fitness across a wide range of domains, including cardiovascular endurance, stamina, strength, flexibility, power, speed, coordination, agility, balance, and accuracy.
